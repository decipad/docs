# Operators

Operators are reserved symbols and words that allow you to perform operations in Deci.

#### Symbol operators:

* Plus sign ⇒ `+`
* Minus sign ⇒ `-`
* Multiplication sign ⇒ `x` `*`
* Division sign ⇒ `/`
* Exponentiation sign ⇒ `**`
* Square root ⇒ `sqrt( )`
* Equal to ⇒ `=`
* Greater than ⇒ `>`
* Greater than or equal to ⇒ `>=`
* Less than ⇒ `<`
* Less than or equal to ⇒ `<=`
* For numbers without units:
  * Equal to ⇒ `==`
  * Not equal to ⇒ `!=`
* For numbers with units:
  * Equal to ⇒ `===`
  * Not equal to ⇒ `!==`

#### Word operators:

* `if`
* `in`
* `by`
* `per`
* `from`
* `to`
* `import`
* `through`
