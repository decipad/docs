# Operators

Operators are reserved symbols and words that allow you to perform operations in Deci.

#### Symbol operators:

* Plus sign ⇒ `+`
* Minus sign ⇒ `-`
* Multiplication sign ⇒ `*`
* Division sign ⇒ `/`
* Exponentiation sign ⇒ `**`
* Equal to ⇒ `=`
* Greater than ⇒ `>`
* Greater than or equal to ⇒ `>=`
* Less than ⇒ `<`
* Less than or equal to ⇒ `<=`

#### Word operators:

* `through`
* `by`
